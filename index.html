Hi! I'm Dina Lamdany

I live in San Francisco, and work as a product manager at Google on the Identity team. Last year, I lived in New York and worked on the Digital Analytics team at Hillary for America. In my free time, I read many things, and write some, too.


I'm interested in the sociology of technology, as well as civil rights, criminal justice, and campaign finance reform, among a variety of domestic policy issues. I like the internet, and love the world that holds it.
