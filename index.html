Hi! I'm Dina Lamdany

I'm currently on the move and looking for fun projects, so feel free to reach out!


I will be starting work as an Associate Product Manager at Google this summer, and most recently lived in New York and worked on the Digital Analytics team at Hillary for America. In my free time, I read many things, and write some, too.


I recently graduated from Columbia with a degree in computer science. Previously, I served as one of the leaders of ADI, Columbia's tech club, and worked as an intern at Google and Twilio.


I'm interested in the sociology of technology, as well as civil rights, criminal justice, and campaign finance reform, among a variety of domestic policy issues. I like the internet, and love the world that holds it.
